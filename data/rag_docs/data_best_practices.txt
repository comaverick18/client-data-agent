ENTERPRISE DATA DICTIONARY — BEST PRACTICES REFERENCE
Data & Analytics Team
Last revised: 2025-Q4

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. FIELD NAMING CONVENTIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
All field names must follow snake_case formatting with no abbreviations,
acronyms, or truncated words. The goal is for a field name to be
self-describing without requiring a separate data dictionary lookup.

  Correct:   company_name, contract_value, renewal_date
  Incorrect: co_name, ContractVal, renewDt, ARR, cust_id

Multi-word concepts must use full words joined by underscores:
  account_owner (not acct_owner or accountOwner)
  resolution_time_hours (not res_time or resolutionTime)

Boolean fields must be prefixed with "is_" or "has_":
  is_churned, has_sso_enabled

Timestamp fields must be suffixed with "_at" for event-based fields
and "_date" for calendar-based fields:
  created_at, updated_at, renewal_date, order_date

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2. REQUIRED FIELDS BY SOURCE TYPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CRM (e.g. Salesforce):
  client_id         — unique identifier, non-null, system-generated
  company_name      — legal entity name, non-null
  industry          — vertical classification (standardised picklist)
  contract_value    — annual recurring revenue in USD, numeric, non-null
  renewal_date      — ISO 8601 date (YYYY-MM-DD), non-null
  account_owner     — internal owner name or employee ID, non-null
  health_score      — numeric 0–100 composite score
  subscription_tier — enum: Starter | Growth | Enterprise

Order Management System:
  order_id          — unique identifier, non-null, system-generated
  client_id         — foreign key to CRM client_id, non-null
  company_name      — denormalised for join resilience
  order_date        — ISO 8601 date, non-null
  product_name      — full product name (no SKU abbreviations)
  quantity          — integer, non-null, minimum 1
  unit_price        — numeric USD, non-null
  total_value       — computed: quantity × unit_price, non-null
  status            — enum: pending | processing | delivered | cancelled

Support / Ticketing (e.g. Zendesk):
  ticket_id         — unique identifier, non-null, system-generated
  client_id         — foreign key to CRM client_id, non-null
  company_name      — denormalised for join resilience
  created_date      — ISO 8601 datetime, non-null
  priority          — enum: low | medium | high | critical
  category          — enum: billing | technical | onboarding | feature_request
  status            — enum: open | pending | closed
  resolution_time_hours — numeric, null if ticket is open
  assigned_agent    — internal agent name or ID, non-null

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3. UNIVERSAL DATA QUALITY RULES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ID fields (client_id, order_id, ticket_id):
  - Must be non-null in every row
  - Must be unique within their source system
  - Must use a consistent prefix format (e.g. C001, ORD-10041, TKT-4801)

Date and timestamp fields:
  - Must conform to ISO 8601: YYYY-MM-DD for dates, YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SSZ for datetimes
  - Mixed formats (MM/DD/YYYY, epoch timestamps, relative strings) are not acceptable
  - Null dates are only permitted in fields explicitly marked nullable (e.g. resolved_at)

Currency and numeric fields:
  - Must be stored as numeric types (float or integer), never as formatted strings
  - No currency symbols, commas, or units embedded in the value (e.g. 84000, not "$84,000")
  - Zero and null are semantically different — do not substitute one for the other

Text fields:
  - company_name must be normalised to title case before ingestion
  - Trailing whitespace, leading punctuation, and trailing punctuation (periods, commas)
    must be stripped during ETL
  - Empty strings ("") are treated as null; do not use placeholder values like "N/A" or "Unknown"
